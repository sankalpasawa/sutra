The problem

You have an idea. Now what?

You open your terminal. You start coding. Three hours later, you've built a login page that nobody asked for, you haven't validated your idea, and you don't know if anyone wants this.

Building a product is not the hard part. Knowing WHAT to build, for WHO, and WHY... that's what kills startups. Most founders skip this. Then they spend months building the wrong thing.

"The biggest risk is not building the wrong feature. It's building the wrong product."

How Sutra works

8 phases. 60 minutes. One command.

Sutra walks you through a structured process. Not a template. A thinking partner that adapts to your specific idea, market, and skills.

1

Intake

Sutra asks you 10 sharp questions. Who is this for? What's the bet? What's the smallest version? No hand-waving allowed.

5 min
2

Market Research

Sutra researches your competitors, finds user complaints, discovers existing APIs and datasets. Your decisions are now data-informed, not guesses.

10 min
3

Shape

PR/FAQ test. Feature carve (ruthless). Risk map. Success metrics. Your fuzzy idea becomes a clear, one-page brief.

10 min
4

Decide

Commit or kill. Is the bet clear? Is the scope small enough? Is this worth your time? No building before this gate.

2 min
5

Architect

Sutra classifies your product type, picks the right tech stack for YOUR skills, generates your data model, and plans deployment. Every choice has a reason.

15 min
6

Configure

Sutra generates a custom operating system for your company. Not a template. A fitted OS based on your product type, platform, stage, and team size.

10 min
7

Deploy

Your company folder is created. OS files written. Committed to git. Your company now exists.

5 min
8

Activate

Project initialized with a roadmap. First feature ready to plan. You're building within minutes of finishing onboarding.

5 min

How Sutra thinks

Company-first, not code-first

Most AI tools help you write code faster. Sutra helps you build a company. The difference: code is a means, not the end. Sutra makes sure you're building the right thing before you build it fast.

Sense

Understand the situation

Check production health. Review learnings from past sessions. Understand what's working and what's not.

Shape

Turn fuzzy into clear

Brainstorm, research, carve scope. Every idea gets stress-tested against market reality.

Decide

Commit or kill

Architecture review. Design review. Second opinions. No building without commitment.

Build

Execute with quality

Plan. Execute in waves. Test. Fix. Review code. Ship. Monitor. Learn.
